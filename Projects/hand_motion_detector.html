Motion Tracking

As the hands more, the palm of the hand is measured and recorded. As the last 50 points are displayed on the screen, a post process filters connected points only. If two points are too far apart (which occurs when the hands become swapped or another hand appears abruptly), a line is not draw. This provides a cleaner output of motion tracking for a maximum of two hands.

Algorithm

1. Input video and output video handlers
2. Initialize 2 empty lists, to store previous hand-positions
3. For each frame grabbed:
 4. Convert frame to RBG
 5. Using pretrained hand-detection mp_hand_gesture, detect hands in frame
 6. Detect palm of hand, and add to appropriate hand-list
 7. Add point to curve of detected hand
 8. Draw curve of previous palm-locations
  9. Limit size of these lists to 75 data-points per hand
  10. Two points are considered sequential if they are within 75px of each other
 11. Display and Save Frame for output
